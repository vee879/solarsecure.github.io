About Us

Get your Solar System Now

All of our solar packages include high quality solar panels, an efficient inverter, a roof mounting system and complete installation by a qualified electrician and CEC member. We also handle all of the paper work, including applying for your feed in tariff connection.

There has never been a better time to install a solar system due to the generous benefits established by the State Government. There are two different rebate systems, one helps to lower your initial purchase price and the other increases the amount of money your solar system generates. The first is called the Renewable Energy Credits which are created when an energy generation system is installed. The second is called the Gross/Net Feed in Tariff.

Who We Are

Solar Secure is a company registered in New South Wales offering solar systems lead by experience management. Solar secure mission is to become the leading solar company offering the highest quality products and customer service while saving customers money and making positive contributions to our environment. Solar secure is being lead by experience management who has both the education and experience in the solar industry for over 5 Years.

Solar Secure use products that are manufactured by a leading industry suppliers and bought from local distributors located in Australia. With this procurement/manufacturing method, Solar Secure is able to minimize large capital expenditures for manufacturing while being able to offer high quality products.

Solar Secure provides a 10 years Structural warranty on their products against manufacturer's defects. Five years is the industry standard. This requirement ensures that Solar secure solar system are installed properly and minimizes the risk of decreasing brand equity due to faulty installation.
